Profile

"A geek philosopher", these are the three words that best describe me.

What does it mean? I am passionate about understanding and learning in general and about coding and data in particular.

As a trained research scientist, I like to analyze complex problems and brainstorm hypotheses about possible solutions. Once I have a good hypothesis, then I cannot wait to test it. To do so, I design experiments for collecting data on which I can apply statistical models and get better insights of the problem.
Importantly, I continuously seek for alternative perspectives/technologies to find better solutions or new ways of innovating.
